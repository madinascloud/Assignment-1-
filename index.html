About NYUAD Bookstore

Ever wondered where you can find a piece of NYUAD to take with you? The campus bookstore is that place. From textbooks to cozy NYUAD hoodies and stationery. Whether you are looking for study supplies or a little souvenir to remember your time here, the bookstore is full of surprises waiting to be discovered.
